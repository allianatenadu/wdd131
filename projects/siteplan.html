Local Artist Spotlight - Website Planning Document

Site Name

ArtistSpotlight

Domain consideration: artistspotlight.local or local-artist-spotlight.com

I chose "ArtistSpotlight" because it clearly communicates the website's purpose of highlighting and featuring local artists. The name is memorable, professional, and immediately tells visitors what to expect. It emphasizes the celebratory nature of showcasing creative talent while maintaining a professional tone that artists would be proud to be associated with.

Site Purpose

The Local Artist Spotlight website serves as a comprehensive digital gallery and community hub for local artists. Its primary purposes include:

  • Providing a professional online platform for local artists to showcase their portfolios
  • Creating a searchable directory of artists categorized by medium and style
  • Offering interview features and artist stories to connect creators with their audience
  • Maintaining an up-to-date calendar of local art events, exhibitions, and workshops
  • Facilitating connections between artists, collectors, and art enthusiasts through a contact system

Scenarios

Question 1: "I'm looking for a local photographer for my wedding. How can I find photographers in my area and see examples of their work?"
Question 2: "I'm an emerging artist who just moved to the area. How can I get my work featured on your website and connect with the local art community?"
Question 3: "What art exhibitions or gallery openings are happening this weekend that I could attend?"

Color Schema

Primary (#2C3E50): Deep Blue-Gray - Used for main headings, navigation bar, and footer
Secondary (#E74C3C): Vibrant Red - Used for call-to-action buttons, links, and important accents
Accent (#F39C12): Warm Orange - Used for hover effects, highlights, and secondary buttons
Background (#ECEFF1): Light Gray - Used for page background and section separators

Typography

Playfair Display (Serif)

Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3) to create an elegant, artistic feel that complements the creative nature of the website.

Open Sans (Sans-serif)

Used for body text, navigation menus, and general content to ensure excellent readability and a clean, modern appearance.
