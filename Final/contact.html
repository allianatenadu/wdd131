Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my artwork for consideration?

You can submit your artwork through our artist submission form on the homepage. We review all submissions and will contact you within 2-3 business days if your work is selected for our platform.

Is there a fee to be featured as an artist?

No, Local Artist Spotlight is completely free for artists. We believe in supporting our creative community without financial barriers.

How can I promote my event on your platform?

Use our event submission form to share details about your art-related event. We feature exhibitions, workshops, gallery openings, and community art events.

Can I purchase artwork directly through your site?

While we don't handle transactions directly, each artist profile includes contact information so you can connect with them about purchasing their work or commissioning pieces.

How often do you update the artist and event listings?

We review and update our listings weekly, with new artists and events added regularly. Follow us on social media for the most current updates!

Do you offer partnership opportunities for businesses?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with local businesses, galleries, and art organizations. Please use the contact form above and select "Collaboration Opportunity" to discuss partnership possibilities.
